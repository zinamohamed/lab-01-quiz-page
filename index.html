All you need to know about the mad man with a box.

Doctor Who is a TV show that has aired on BBC since November 1963. The doctor is a time-traveling Time Lord from the planet Gallifrey. He makes his way through all of space and time in a spaceship/time machine shaped like a British police box called the TARDIS; which is short for Time and Relative Dimension in Space. The Doctor has a habit of picking up travelling companions such as Rorandicus Pondicus as they set off to time-wobbling adventures. With hundreds of episodes to choose from, getting into Doctor Who can be a little intimidating. But the truth is that most episodes are pretty self-contained. Make your way through seasons of timely-wobbly magic on any streaming platform, but be sure to remember Rule #1.......the doctor always lies.
